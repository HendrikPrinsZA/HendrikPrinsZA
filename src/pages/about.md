Technical lead based in Amsterdam, Netherlands. Passionate about development and obsessed with optimising inefficiencies and automating redundancies.

My career started in 2013 as a software developer at Customer Loyalty Consultants, an insurance service solutions provider focused on efficiency, accuracy, and compliance in critical business systems.

In 2014 I joined CLEVVA, a company specialising in the application of technology to empower people. There I progressed to lead DevOps engineer — responsible for CI/CD, infrastructure, product design, and execution — before relocating to the Netherlands in 2021.

Since then I have worked across hospitality, commodities, and fintech — most recently as technical lead at Finstack, where we help scale-ups speed up month-end closing with automated reporting, consolidation, and collaboration.

## Objective

Aspire to play a positive part in the augmented intelligence revolution, becoming a regular contributor in the open source community and cultivating a trustworthy online footprint. Maintain a hands-on role in the development environment and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in the industry.
